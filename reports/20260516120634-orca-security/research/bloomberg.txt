## Face the data. Change the world.

Data is reality. If you face it, you can understand it. Then, you can do something about it. We’ve taken everything we learned from our business and we’ve put it into [our philanthropic efforts](https://www.bloomberg.org/) — along with almost all of our profits — to help save and improve lives across the globe.
