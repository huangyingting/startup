Petroleum operations span vast geographic areas with critical infrastructure including pipelines, refineries, offshore platforms, and distribution networks. These interconnected systems control essential energy supplies, making them attractive targets for nation-state actors, ransomware groups, and others seeking economic or geopolitical impact.

Attacks can disrupt production at wellheads, compromise pipeline operations, halt refinery processes, or impact distribution networks. Even IT-focused ransomware can force operational shutdowns as a safety precaution, affecting energy supply chains and causing significant economic impact.

Common targets include SCADA systems controlling pipelines, DCS in refineries, safety instrumented systems, and protocols like Modbus, DNP3, and OPC. Remote operations often rely on satellite communications and wireless networks that require specialized security approaches.

Our solutions are designed for challenging connectivity environments including offshore platforms and remote wellheads. We understand satellite network limitations and provide security that works within operational constraints while maintaining protection effectiveness.

Focus on network segmentation between IT and OT, secure remote access controls, risk-based vulnerability management, and continuous network monitoring. Implement backup and recovery procedures tested for your specific operational environment.
