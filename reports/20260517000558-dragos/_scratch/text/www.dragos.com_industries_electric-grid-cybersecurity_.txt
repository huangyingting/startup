Electric grid cybersecurity protects power generation, transmission, and distribution systems from cyber threats that could disrupt electricity supply or damage critical infrastructure.

Operational technology (OT) systems control critical power infrastructure. Cyber attacks on OT networks can cause power outages, equipment damage, and threats to public safety.

Successful cyber attacks can shut down power plants, trip transmission lines, cause cascading blackouts, and damage expensive equipment, resulting in billions in economic losses.

NERC CIP standards mandate cybersecurity controls for bulk electric system operators, including asset identification, security controls, incident reporting, and personnel training.

Our platform provides comprehensive visibility, threat detection, and incident response capabilities specifically designed for electric grid OT environments, helping utilities detect and respond to threats quickly.

Ready to enhance your cybersecurity posture? Our experts will assess your current security and recommend solutions tailored to your electric utility operations.
